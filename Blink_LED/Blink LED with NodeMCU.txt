# Blink LED with NodeMCU

## Description
A simple project to blink an LED using NodeMCU. Helps you learn GPIO output and basic Arduino programming.

## Components Required
- NodeMCU ESP8266
- LED
- 220Î© Resistor
- Breadboard & Jumper Wires

## Circuit / Connections
- Connect the LED anode (+) to D5 through a resistor.
- Connect LED cathode (-) to GND.

## How to Use
1. Upload `Blink_LED.ino` to NodeMCU.
2. LED will blink ON and OFF every 500ms automatically.
